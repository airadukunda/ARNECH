Our Services

Research Design & Implementation

We design and execute comprehensive research studies using cutting-edge methodologies. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures robust findings that inform policy and practice across various sectors including health, education, and social sciences.

Strategic Consulting

Our expert consultants provide strategic guidance to organizations seeking to improve their projects, implement evidence-based practices, and navigate complex challenges in today's rapidly evolving landscape.

Data Analytics & Insights

We transform complex data into actionable insights using advanced analytical techniques. Our team specializes in quantitative and qualitative analysis, helping clients make informed decisions based on solid evidence.

Policy Analysis & Development

We analyze existing policies and develop new frameworks that address contemporary challenges. Our policy expertise spans multiple sectors, with particular strength in public health, education, and social policy.

Capacity Building & Training

We build institutional capacity through customized training programs, workshops, and mentorship initiatives. Our approach strengthens local capabilities and ensures sustainable impact.

Grant Writing & Funding Support

Our experienced team assists organizations in securing funding through compelling grant proposals and funding strategies. We have a proven track record of successful applications to major funding agencies.

Our Research Units

Monitoring & Evaluation Unit

Specializes in M&E framework development, impact evaluation, and program assessment. Designs effective systems for routine data collection and analysis.

Disease Modeling & Epidemiology Unit

Specialized in mathematical modeling of infectious diseases including Measles, Malaria, and Tuberculosis. Focuses on real-time outbreak analysis and epidemic forecasting for public health decision-making.

Vaccine Research & Evaluation Unit

Dedicated to vaccine effectiveness studies, optimal vaccination strategies development, and immunization program evaluation for disease control and elimination efforts.

Biostatistics & Data Science Unit

Advanced statistical analysis and AI applications in health research. Expertise in data management, statistical modeling, and machine learning approaches for global health solutions.

Global Health Policy Unit

Focuses on health systems strengthening, policy analysis, and international health governance. Provides evidence-based policy recommendations for health sector improvements.

Digital Health Innovation Unit

Develops digital health solutions, health information systems, and innovative technologies for improving healthcare delivery in resource-limited settings.

Economic and Social Business Unit

Focuses on economic analysis, social business development, and sustainable financing models. Expertise in financial inclusion, poverty reduction strategies, and socio-economic impact assessments.
